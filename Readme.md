# 📊 BlinkIT Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze BlinkIT's grocery sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights using interactive visualizations, KPIs, and DAX calculations, enabling data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To conduct a comprehensive analysis of BlinkIT's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to identify trends, improve operational efficiency, and support strategic business decisions.

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1. Total Sales by Fat Content

* Donut Chart
* Compare sales of Low Fat and Regular products.

### 2. Total Sales by Item Type

* Horizontal Bar Chart
* Identify top-performing product categories.

### 3. Fat Content by Outlet

* Stacked Bar Chart
* Compare outlet performance based on fat content.

### 4. Outlet Establishment Analysis

* Area/Line Chart
* Analyze sales based on outlet establishment year.

### 5. Sales by Outlet Size

* Donut Chart
* Compare Medium, Small, and High outlet performance.

### 6. Sales by Outlet Location

* Bar Chart
* Compare sales across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities.

### 7. Outlet Type Analysis

* Matrix Visualization
* Compare Total Sales, Total Items, Average Sales, Ratings, and Item Visibility across outlet types.

---

## 📌 Business Insights

* Supermarket Type 1 generated the highest overall sales.
* Tier 3 locations contributed the highest revenue among all outlet locations.
* Medium-sized outlets accounted for the largest share of total sales.
* Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods were the highest-selling product categories.
* Low Fat products generated slightly higher revenue than Regular products.
* Customer ratings remained consistently high across all outlet types.
